Our Story

From render to reality.

Temecula Interior Decor grew out of a simple request we heard again and again: “Can we just buy the pieces you use?”

As an interior design studio, we spend our days sourcing furniture that is well-proportioned, genuinely comfortable, and quietly modern — and we bring the best of it home under our own name. Every piece in the collection carries the Temecula Interior Decor Furniture label: modern furniture, chosen with the same eye we bring to our client interiors and delivered directly to you.

Prefer a hand? Our designers can help you select, place, and style each piece into the room it’s meant for.
